1. Introduction

Welcome to Calculator App ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculator web application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Important: This is a client-side web application. All data processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not have servers that collect, store, or process your personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our calculator application is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not use tracking cookies, analytics services, or collect usage statistics.
  • No Server Storage: All calculations and vault content remain in your browser's local storage.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any.

3. Local Storage Usage

The application uses your browser's local storage for:

  • Vault PIN: A hashed version of your 4-digit PIN (not the actual PIN)
  • Wrong Attempt Counter: Number of incorrect PIN attempts for security lockouts
  • Google Drive Links: URLs to content you choose to add to your vault
  • Calculator Memory: Current calculation state (temporary)

All local storage data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

4. Google Drive Integration

4.1 How It Works

When you use the vault feature with Google Drive:

  • We store only the links to your Google Drive files, not the files themselves
  • File access is controlled by Google Drive's sharing settings that you configure
  • We cannot access, modify, or delete files from your Google Drive
  • Deleting content from the vault only removes the link, not the file from Google Drive

4.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Setting appropriate sharing permissions in Google Drive
  • Managing access to your Google Drive files
  • Understanding Google's privacy policies for Drive
  • Regularly reviewing your Google Drive security settings
Security Note: The vault feature provides basic front-end protection. For highly sensitive content, we recommend using Google Drive's built-in security features and considering physical privacy measures.

5. Security Features

Our application includes these security measures:

  • PIN Protection: 4-digit PIN with progressive lockout delays
  • Screenshot Deterrence: Front-end techniques to discourage casual screenshots
  • Local Processing: All sensitive operations occur in your browser
  • Auto-Logout: Automatic logout on multiple security violations

Important Limitations: Complete screenshot prevention is technically impossible in web browsers. These are deterrents, not guarantees.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect any personal information, this is inherently protected.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated is:

  • Google Drive: For optional vault content storage

When using Google Drive integration, you are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing Google's policies at policies.google.com/privacy.

8. Your Rights and Control

Since all data remains on your device:

  • You can clear all application data by clearing your browser's local storage
  • You can change your vault PIN at any time through the admin panel
  • You can add or remove Google Drive content as needed
  • You can choose not to use the vault feature entirely
